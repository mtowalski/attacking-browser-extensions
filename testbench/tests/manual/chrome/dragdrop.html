Link Drag & Drop

Instead of being clicked, links can be dragged to the navigation bar to navigate to them. In some cases it works and in some it doesn't.

  • Drag me to your navigation bar (chrome-extension pointing to extension) . This works (even if resources are not web-accessible).
  • Drag me to your navigation bar (chrome-extension pointing to app) . This does not work.
  • Drag me to your navigation bar (file) . This does not work.
  • Drag the content of this input box to the navigation bar (chrome-extension pointing to extension): . This works.
  • Drag the content of this input box to the navigation bar (chrome-extension pointing to app): . This does not work.
  • Drag the content of this input box to the navigation bar (file): . This works.
