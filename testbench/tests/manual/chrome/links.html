Link Interaction

While normally clicking either of the two links below is blocked by Chrome, a right click and "Open in a new Tab" or "Open in a new window" works for the first one. Note, that <Ctrl+Click> or <Ctrl+Shift+Click> does not work although you might think it invokes the exact same functionality.

  • Right click me (chrome-extension pointing to extension) . This works (even if resources are not web-accessible).
  • Right click me (chrome-extension pointing to app) . This does not work.
  • Right click me (file) . This does not work.
